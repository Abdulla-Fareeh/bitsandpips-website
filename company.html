Bits & Pips Maldives

We are a registered software development and consultancy firm based in Male', Maldives, dedicated to building the next generation of secure, decentralized applications.

Our Mission

Bits & Pips was founded with a singular objective: to restore privacy and connectivity in an increasingly centralized world.

Combining advanced scientific research (M4) with enterprise-grade computer programming (J5), we build software that respects user sovereignty. Our flagship product, EchoLAN, represents our commitment to "Local First" technology—software that works perfectly without the cloud.
