Why EchoLAN?

Offline Magic

Discover and connect with nearby devices on your Wi-Fi using Bonjour—no internet, no hassle. Ideal for teams, events, or spotty connections.

Lightning Transfers

Share files and media at full LAN speeds—up to 10x faster than cloud services, with zero data leaving your network.

Zero-Knowledge Privacy

End-to-end encryption ensures chats and calls stay between devices. No servers, no tracking—just pure, private communication.

Simple & Secure

Easy user discovery with accept/decline prompts, plus built-in blocking and optional email reporting for seamless support.
